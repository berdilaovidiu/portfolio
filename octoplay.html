OctoPlay is a game platform dedicated to children with speech impairments, parents and therapists. It is co-developed with domain experts in SLP and ASD and leverages AR and VR to keep children engaged and focused.

There are two games, one that focuses mainly on short term memory and another one that focuses on pronouncing sounds.

My Role
