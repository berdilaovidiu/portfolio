I am a designer with a strong technical background in software systems and a passion for making technology friendly.

I picked up the interest in design in 2011, while attending a cognitive engineering seminar hosted by the company I was writing software for at that time. Since then, I have slowly transitioned into a design role, while working on complex and interesting user interfaces in the financial, bio informatics, IoT and real estate industries.

Personally, I envision a future where technology enriches people's lives, by simplifying interactions, strengthening social bonds and communities and guiding people to focus on things that truly matter. As an old man, I hope to proudly look back thinking I had a part in all this.
