Hello, I am a Java developer looking to get involved more in the beautiful field of User Experience Design.
I currently live in Cluj Napoca, Romania and I am enjoying every bit of fun this beautiful city has to offer, except the weather that I sometimes hate, especially during the winters when it gets very cold, so cold that the local flasher was once caught describing himself to women.
Being involved for a while in the software development industry and seeing the lack of knowledge and interest most of the companies show for the UX domain really motivates me to make a change.
I am surrounded by smart individuals with passion for technology and it is sometimes painful to hear users complain about poor user experiences with the products we develop. I really think we can do more to earn the respect and appreciation of our users and more than that, make technology less scary for non technical people.
Personally, I envision a future where everybody uses technology to make their lives less complicated and more enjoyable and I will proudly look back twenty years from now, thinking that I had a part in all this.
I picked up the interest in design while attending a UI/UX seminar, back in 2011. I was then working for a company that produces software for the financial industry. They brought in a design expert from the United States to teach a class about user-centered design. Since then, I have been working on complex and interesting user interfaces for both financial and bioinformatics industries.
When I am not working, I like to travel, read, dance and watch sports. I particularly like snooker, combat sports and most recently, Formula 1 racing.
