I strive to design simple and elegant solutions to complex problems

As a product designer with a strong technical background in software systems, I am passionate about making technology accessible and user-friendly. My interest in design was sparked during a cognitive engineering seminar I attended in 2011 while working as a front-end developer.

Since then, I have gradually transitioned into a design role, gaining experience designing complex user interfaces in a variety of industries, including finance, bioinformatics, IoT, real estate, gig economy, education and eCommerce.

My personal vision is for technology to enrich people's lives by simplifying interactions, strengthening social bonds and communities, and guiding people to focus on what truly matters. As I look towards the future, I hope to continue contributing to this vision and take pride in the part I played in making it a reality.
