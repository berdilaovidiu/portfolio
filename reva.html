Reva is a vertically integrated CRM for the US multifamily real estate market. The project started in 2015 when an industry player reached out to a group of Silicon Valley entrepreneurs to co-develop a solution that leverages new technological advancements to address usability and reliability issues and gather actionable analytics.

Team setup

I was part of a very experienced product team of six. We coordinated with engineering teams on three continents. Most of the product team was on the US west coast and engineering was spread across Romania, Costa Rica and Peru. I collaborated remotely with the product team while acting as a liaison between Product and the Romanian engineering teams.

My role

I started as a UX designer, after a very thorough interview. In the beginning there was a lot of wireframing and design critique as we were laying the foundations of the design system and covering the main flows. The role quickly evolved as I began to write engineering stories, plan engineering sprints and validate implementations. As I became more familiar with the business and user needs, product vision and technological constraints, I took on more complex assignments that culminated with me managing Contract Renewals, a large and complex feature that took over a year to design, develop and release.

For a brief period I took on customer support responsibilities. Being closer to users and customers really helped me gain a better understanding of whom I was designing for and what the priorities were.
