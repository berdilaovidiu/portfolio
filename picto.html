Picto
Uber for photography

Picto is a mobile marketplace for on demand photography. It is meant for families, travelers and social media active people, on occasions when a selfie is simply not enough. The idea is to hail a nearby photographer for a micro photo shoot with instant delivery at an affordable cost.

My Role

My main role was to manage the product. I was heavily involved in validating the need, the technological and the business assumptions. Once we moved on to implementation, I managed the engineering sprints and started developing relationships with partners (photographers, festival organizers, PR companies, social media influencers and marketing agencies). When we got close to launch, I started developing relationships with the local startup ecosystem and began pitching the idea to angel investors.

My secondary role was to lead the design effort. Early on I was in charge of wireframing, prototyping and user testing of the solution. Later on I led the branding effort and worked closely with our visual designers for pixel perfect mockups, marketing materials and landing pages.
